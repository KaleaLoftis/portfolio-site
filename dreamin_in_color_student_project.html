Fall 2022

View on Behance

Project Overview

For my final project for the ASU fall 2022 GIT 303 Digital Design course, I designed print collateral materials for a conference of my choice. I produced a multi-page conference program (brochure), table tent cards, conference badges, and a sign-up teaser ad. As part of my project parameters, I had to research the conference's previous events. Given the choice to build off the current conference's brand style or create a new spin on the brand, I was asked to make a complete and visually connected conference material pack. I had to represent a comprehensive knowledge of skills related to layout, design theory, understanding of pages, printing, typography, images, colors, layer effects, and expertise in exporting.
